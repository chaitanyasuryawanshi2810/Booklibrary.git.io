Genre Categories

Fiction

Stories and narratives created from the author's imagination. Includes novels, short stories, and plays. Subgenres often include Fantasy, Thriller, and Romance.

Non-Fiction

Literature based on facts, real events, real people, and verifiable information. Examples include essays and journalism.

Biography

An account of someone's life written by another person. A Memoir or Autobiography is written by the subject themselves.

Self-Help

Books intended to instruct readers on solving personal problems or improving their lives, often focusing on psychology or finance.

Science

Texts exploring various fields of scientific study, including physics, biology, and chemistry. Can be academic or popular science.

History

Detailed accounts and analysis of past events, usually presented in chronological order and based on factual evidence.
