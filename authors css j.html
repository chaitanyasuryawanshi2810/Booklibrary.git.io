✍️ Meet Our Featured Authors

Explore the lives and works of some of the most influential authors featured in our library collection.

Author Spotlights

Paulo Coelho

Nationality: Brazilian | Born: 1947
A highly popular novelist and lyricist, famous for his mystical and allegorical writing style. His works often explore themes of destiny, personal legends, and the spiritual journey of life.

Notable Work: The Alchemist

Dr. A.P.J. Abdul Kalam

Nationality: Indian | Lived: 1931-2015
A renowned scientist and the 11th President of India. Known as the "Missile Man of India," his writings focus on science, technology, youth empowerment, and inspirational leadership.

Notable Work: Wings of Fire

J.K. Rowling

Nationality: British | Born: 1965
A fantasy author, screenwriter, and producer best known for creating the globally famous Harry Potter book series. Her imagination has profoundly shaped modern young adult fiction.

Notable Work: Harry Potter and the Philosopher's Stone
