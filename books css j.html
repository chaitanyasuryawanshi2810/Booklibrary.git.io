Our Top Picks

The Alchemist

Author: Paulo Coelho Genre: Philosophical Fiction | Published: 1988

A shepherd boy named Santiago travels from Spain to the Egyptian desert in search of a hidden treasure. This allegorical novel is about realizing one's Personal Legend and the importance of following your heart.

Wings of Fire

Author: Dr. A.P.J. Abdul Kalam with Arun Tiwari Genre: Autobiography, Inspirational | Published: 1999

The inspiring autobiography of the former President of India, detailing his early life and work in space research. It is a powerful narrative of determination and belief.

Rich Dad Poor Dad

Author: Robert Kiyosaki Genre: Personal Finance | Published: 1997

A book that advocates the importance of financial literacy and building assets. It contrasts the lessons learned from two fathers on wealth creation.
